The Johnstown Castle Hotel

Best Rate Guarantee | Free Wifi | No Booking Fee | Free Parking | Free Cancellation

The Johnstown Castle Hotel is a 4-star award winning luxury hotel where victorian splendour meets modern day luxury. Situated on an estate with a a long and colourful history stretching over 800 years. Located only a few miles outside of Wexford town in Ireland's south east, it is a place of great romantic charm and tranquility with spectacular lake walks and walled gardens. The current building is built in the Gothic Revival style but original building dates all the way back to the 12th century.

The Castle has 192 deluxe bedrooms, a business conference centre which can accomodate up to 500 delegates and an exquisite wedding venue in the charming classical style but with all modern comforts. The castle grounds contain acres of beautifully restored gardens that make the perfect backdrop for capturing those precious wedding memories.

Situated in Ireland's famous Sunny South East region the Castle is also home to our award winning Spa Leisure Centre with a 25 metre pool and state of the art Gym. Our spa was created to offer an atmosphere and elegance with lots of treatment rooms and a wide variety of treatments available

In addition, the hotel is also home to the The Moat which is set in the Castle's original dining room. Our team of top trained chefs have designed a menu full of flavour with organic ingredients from local and sustainable sources.

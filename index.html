About

The Johnstown Castle Hotel is a 4-star award winning luxury hotel where victorian splendour meets modern day luxury. Situated on an estate with a a long and colourful history stretching over 800 years. Only a few miles outside of Wexford town in Ireland's south east, it is a place of great romantic charm and tranquility with spectacular lake walks and walled gardens. The current building is built in the Gothic Revival style but original building dates back to the 12th century.
